Artificial Intelligence

I am interested in AI, and I have been experimenting with ChatGPT. This page contains chat logs from my interactions with ChatGPT.

For most of these logs, I was communicating with ChatGPT using voice-to-text, and so the text translation of what I said is sometimes inaccurate. For this reason, and also to provide context for many of the things I said, I have added comments thoughout the chat logs. These comments are clearly marked to distingush them from the original chat logs.

Each chat log has also been divided into parts, with a name given to each part. These names are generally more useful than the title of the page itself, which is usually an autogenerated title created by ChatGPT.

All of my interactions with ChatGPT are included here, with the exception of a handful of chats that I had before I signed up for an account, as well as one chat which mysteriously disappeared from the ChatGPT interface. There are also a few versions of "Circuits of the Heart" that I generated without saving them.
