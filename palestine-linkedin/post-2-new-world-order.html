New World Order [back]

I originally called the second post "New World Order". When I named it, I felt it was just a cool way of referring to having a revolution. Then I read this Wikipedia article:

New World Order (conspiracy theory)

It appears the term sometimes refers to specific conspiracy theory about a secretly emerging totalitarian world government. Since I was unaware of this I found it somewhat alarming. I am not familiar with this specific conspiracy theory.

At least, however, there is this use, also on Wikipedia:

New world order (politics)

I renamed the post to "After the Revolution" just in case. It still makes sense with that title.

My thinking on a revolution is pretty straightforward. Elsewhere on this website I talked about printing a currency. I think we need some central entity that manages and distributes a world currency in a fair way, so that ethical incentives can be created.
