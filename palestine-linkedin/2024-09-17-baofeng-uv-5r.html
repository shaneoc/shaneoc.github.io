Baofeng UV-5R

Post Date: 2024-09-17
Original Post: LinkedIn

On Al Jazeera they are now reporting that an attack has been performed on Hezbollah members in Lebanon that caused hundreds of pagers to explode simultaneously. They are reporting that 1,200 people have been injured. I have not heard of any deaths, but some people have lost their fingers. I believe I heard them report that the devices were somewhat new and were purchased in the last year, though I am not completely sure.

On my website, below the Al Jazeera feed on the homepage, I have a suggestion that people buy the Baofeng UV-5R:

https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Baofeng_UV-5R

It is not really a replacement for a smartphone, but at least with amateur radio it is easier to build your own device that you can be sure does not contain an explosive mechanism or surveillance device, and it does not require a central tower.
