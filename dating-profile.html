It seems to me like we might be living in a computer simulation which is simulating the events leading up to the AI singularity. I am increasingly thinking that it might be a good idea for me to find a girlfriend who agrees that it is a good idea to prepare for the AI singularity.

Things I find attractive

I am attracted to women. Therefore, for me to find someone physically attractive, there must be things about them that make their gender obvious. Usually women have longer hair and wear distinct types of clothes, and this is sufficient to make their gender obvious. However, in terms of roles, I like it when women are confident and are comfortable doing things that males normally do. If I meet a woman who is interested in technology or computers I find that attractive.

Toronto has a lot of immigrants and so I have become used to dating women of other races. The way I see it, if all else is equal, if a woman is a different race than me, then that makes her slightly more attractive.

Number of girlfriends

I have described elsewhere on this website that I think it would be cool if I could have three girlfriends. I feel I should explain this.

I like the way it sounds to say that I have three girlfriends, because it makes it seem like I am very desirable and I enjoy imagining doing certain activities with three girlfriends. However, it is an aspirational goal. In practice, I suspect that it is difficult to have an exactly equal relationship with three women at the same time. I would also be fine with one girlfriend.
